My Showreel

This is the showreel level that I made in college and was one of my earliest unity projects. It starts as a 2d platformer in the 1st level with a stereotypical boss and enemies with powerups the red powerup is a speed boost and the blue power up is a projectile. In the second level there is a switch in perspective to a 3d world with a sword, primitive climbing and grappling mechanics.
